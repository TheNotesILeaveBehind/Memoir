The Notes I Leave Behind

This is a collection of what I’ve gathered during my time on earth. I am not a person of importance, just a single voice passing through. These are my offerings for anyone becoming.
Remember, breathe deep into life.

Remember the Wolf

We walk through life
pretending the wolf isn’t there.

It keeps its distance
quiet paws,
steady eyes,
no need to chase what is already moving toward it.

We convince ourselves
it will come only when we are old,
when our bones are ready.
As if we decide
when the wolf is allowed to arrive.

But the wolf has never left.
It stalks us from the first breath,
not as a threat,
but as a truth.

Still, we forget.
Until it sinks its teeth
into someone we love.
Then we remember-
and fear it,
curse it,
call it cruel.

But the wolf does not hate.
It does not hunt out of malice.
It simply walks.

To fear the wolf
is to misunderstand the forest.
To pretend it isn't there
is to pretend we do not live.

Let it walk beside you.
Let its presence soften you.
Let it calm you.

One day,
you will lie down
and it will come close-
not to punish,
but to carry you
back into the quiet.

Back to what you were
before you ever ran.

Remember the wolf.

- Anonymous
