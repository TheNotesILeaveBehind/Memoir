The Measure of Respect

True respect from a leader is not born
in your silence,
your nods,
your easy compliance.

It comes when you stand your ground
with calm and courage,
when your voice
carries the weight of conviction
rather than the need to please.

Authority recognizes
not the agreeable,
but the unshaken-
those who will not sway
in the wind of command
without reason.

To challenge with grace
is to invite respect.

You are remembered not by blind agreement,
but by proving you will push back to the authority
if pushed too far.

- Anonymous
